rsl_get_slantr_and_h

Get slant range and height given ground range and elevation.

Syntax

rsl_get_slantr_and_h, ground_range, elevation, slant_range, height

Arguments

Input
ground_range Ground range to radar target, in kilometers.
elevation Elevation angle in degrees.
Output
slant_range Range to radar target, in kilometers.
height Height in kilometers.

Keywords

None
