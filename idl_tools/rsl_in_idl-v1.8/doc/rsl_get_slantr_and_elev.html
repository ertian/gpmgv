rsl_get_slantr_and_elev

Get slant range and elevation given ground range and height.

Syntax

rsl_get_slantr_and_elev, ground_range, height, slant_range, elevation

Arguments

Input
ground_range Ground range to radar target, in kilometers.
height Height in kilometers.
Output
slant_range Range to radar target, in kilometers.
elevation Elevation angle in degrees.

Keywords

None
